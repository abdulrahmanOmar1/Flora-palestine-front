Welcome to the opportunity to join the flora Palestine team!

We are in the process of building an interactive platform aimed at preserving the environmental and cultural heritage of Palestine through education and e-commerce. If you are passionate about nature and committed to preserving the Palestinian identity, you may be one of the people we are looking for.

    What we offer to our team:
  • A stimulating and supportive work environment that values innovation and creativity.
  • Specialized training to enhance your skills and knowledge.
  • Opportunities for professional growth and development within the team.
  • Competitive bonuses that reflect the value and importance of your business.
  • Team activities and events to build strong bonds between our team members.
If you are interested in joining our team and have the necessary passion and experience, we invite you to send your resume and a motivational letter explaining how you can contribute to Flora Palestine's mission.
Thank you for your interest, and we look forward to the possibility of working together to achieve our common goal.
